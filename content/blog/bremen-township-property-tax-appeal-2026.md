## Understanding Your Assessment

Review the notice from the Cook County Assessor's Office, which lists your home's assessed value. Compare similar properties ("comps") in your neighborhood using public records or online valuation tools. If your assessment significantly exceeds nearby home values, you have grounds for appeal.

## Steps to File Your Appeal

1. **Gather evidence:** Recent sales data, condition reports, and photographs.
2. **Complete the form:** Download the 2026 appeal form from the Cook County Board of Review.
3. **Submit before the deadline:** Late appeals are dismissed without review. Check the Board of Review website for your township's exact deadline.
4. **Attend your hearing:** Present your evidence, answer questions, and advocate for a fair value.
