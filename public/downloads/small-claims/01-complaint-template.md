## FILING INSTRUCTIONS

1. Complete this form and make **3 copies** (original for court, 1 for defendant, 1 for your records)
2. File at the Circuit Court Clerk's office in the county where:
   - The defendant lives or does business, OR
   - The incident occurred
3. Pay the filing fee ($75–$200 depending on county and claim amount)
4. The clerk will assign a case number and hearing date
5. Serve the defendant (see Filing Instructions doc for service rules)

---

## COMMON CLAIM TYPES — TIPS

**Security Deposits**
- Illinois law requires return within 30 days (or 45 days if deductions)
- Landlord must provide itemized list of deductions
- If they fail: you may be entitled to 2x the deposit amount

**Contractor / Home Repair Disputes**
- Attach the signed contract, invoices, and photos of the work
- Document all communications (texts, emails count)

**Unpaid Loans Between Individuals**
- Attach any written agreement, Venmo/CashApp records, texts confirming the debt

**Defective Products**
- Keep the product — it's evidence
- Attach receipt, warranty, and any return/refund communications
