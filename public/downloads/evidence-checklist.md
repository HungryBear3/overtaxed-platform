# Evidence Checklist: Illinois Property Tax Appeal

Gather the following documents to support your property tax appeal:

- **Comparable Sales (Comps):**
  - Recent sales of similar properties in your neighborhood within the past 1-3 years.
  - Find on county CCAO website, local real estate sites, or Zillow.
- **Recent Appraisal or Purchase Price:**
  - Valid appraisal document or closing statement as evidence of market value.
  - Contact your lender or search past records if needed.
- **Photos of Property Defects:**
  - Clearly show needed repairs, damage, or issues affecting value.
  - Use smartphone photos and describe the defect.
- **Assessment Errors:**
  - Incorrect lot size, building square footage, bedroom/bathroom count, etc.
  - Check your property card on your county assessor’s website.
- **Neighbor Assessment Comparisons:**
  - Compare your assessment to similar homes on your block.
  - View public assessment data on CCAO or county assessor website.

**Tips:**
- Always print or save official property records.
- Mark up photos and evidence for clarity.
- The more documentation you provide, the stronger your appeal.
