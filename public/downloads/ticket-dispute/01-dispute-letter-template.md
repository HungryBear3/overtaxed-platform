**Date:** [Insert Date]

**To:**
[Name of Hearing Officer / Administrative Adjudication Department]

**Re:** Notice of Contest — Ticket / Notice of Violation No. [Ticket Number]

---

Dear Hearing Officer,

I am writing to formally contest the above-referenced citation issued on [Date of Ticket] at approximately [Time] at or near [Location on Ticket].

**Vehicle Information:**
- License Plate: [Plate Number]
- State: Illinois
- Make/Model: [Year Make Model]

**Grounds for Contest** *(check all that apply — see guide for details):*

☐ **The violation did not occur.** I was not operating the vehicle at the stated time and location, and the citation is factually incorrect.

☐ **Signage was missing, obscured, or incorrect.** The posted sign(s) governing the alleged violation were absent, obstructed, or did not comply with Illinois standards.

☐ **The vehicle was not in violation.** The cited circumstance (e.g., parking zone, time restriction, speed) did not apply to my vehicle at the time of the alleged violation.

☐ **Equipment malfunction / camera error.** The automated enforcement device (red light camera / speed camera) was malfunctioning, improperly calibrated, or the images do not clearly identify my vehicle.

☐ **The vehicle was stolen or operated without my consent.** A police report is attached as supporting documentation.

☐ **Medical emergency.** A physician's documentation is attached explaining the circumstances.

☐ **Other:** [Describe briefly]

---

**Statement of Facts:**

[Write 2–4 sentences in plain language describing what actually happened. Be factual, calm, and specific. Example: "On the date in question, I was traveling southbound on Michigan Avenue within the posted speed limit. The camera image attached to the citation does not clearly show my license plate, and the speed measurement does not match my vehicle's GPS data for that time."]

---

**Relief Requested:**

I respectfully request that this citation be dismissed in its entirety. I am prepared to attend a hearing if required and can provide additional documentation upon request.
