# Questions to Ask Your Illinois Divorce Attorney

**Print this list and bring it to your first consultation.** Attorney time is expensive — walk in prepared.

A good family law attorney consultation covers strategy, timeline, and cost. These questions help you get all three in one meeting.

---

## ABOUT YOUR SITUATION

1. Based on what I've told you, do I qualify for Joint Simplified Dissolution or will I need to file a full petition?

2. How long do you estimate this process will take given our situation?

3. What are the most contested issues you see — where do you expect pushback?

4. Are there any time-sensitive steps I should take right away (before filing)?

5. Should I move out of the marital home, or is it better to stay?

---

## ABOUT PROPERTY & FINANCES

6. How will the marital home likely be handled — sale and split, or buyout?

7. My spouse has a pension / 401(k) from before and during our marriage. How is that divided?

8. I received an inheritance during the marriage — is that considered marital property?

9. We have significant debt (student loans, credit cards, mortgage). Who is responsible for what?

10. My spouse recently transferred money / sold assets. Is that dissipation? What can I do about it?

11. One of us runs a business. How is a business valued and divided in Illinois?

12. What happens to our joint bank accounts right now — should I withdraw anything?

---

## ABOUT CHILDREN

13. What does the standard parenting time schedule look like in this county?

14. How is decision-making authority (education, healthcare, religion) typically allocated?

15. My child is [age] — will their preferences be considered by the court?

16. My spouse is [describe concern: substance abuse, travel for work, mental health]. How does that affect custody?

17. How is child support calculated, and will it change over time?

18. Can I move out of state with the children after the divorce?

---

## ABOUT SPOUSAL SUPPORT

19. Am I (or is my spouse) likely to receive spousal maintenance?

20. Based on the maintenance formula, what is the estimated amount and duration?

21. Can spousal maintenance be modified later if circumstances change?

22. How does maintenance interact with child support?

---

## ABOUT THE PROCESS

23. Will we need to go to court, or can this be handled through mediation / negotiation?

24. What is discovery in my case — will there be depositions or subpoenas?

25. Do you recommend a mediator? Who do you work with?

26. What are the chances this goes to trial? What would trigger that?

27. How long does a trial take and what does it cost?

---

## ABOUT COSTS

28. What is your retainer and hourly rate?

29. What does a case like mine typically cost — best case and worst case?

30. What can I do to keep costs down on my end?

31. Do you bill for emails and phone calls? What is the minimum billing increment?

32. Who else in your office might work on my case, and at what rate?

33. If my spouse has more money, can I ask the court to order them to pay my legal fees?

---

## PROTECTING YOURSELF

34. Should I open individual bank accounts now?

35. Should I remove myself from or monitor joint credit cards?

36. What documents should I gather before we proceed?

37. Is there a risk my spouse will try to hide income or assets? What do we do if that happens?

38. I'm concerned about my spouse's behavior — are there grounds for an emergency order?

---

## LOOKING AHEAD

39. When does the divorce become final and what happens that day?

40. After the judgment, what do I need to update: name, Social Security, beneficiaries, deed, car titles, passport?

41. How do I enforce the agreement if my spouse doesn't comply after the divorce?

42. Can the terms be modified in the future (support, parenting plan)?
